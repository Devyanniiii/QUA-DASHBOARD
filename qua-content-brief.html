What this tool does

From idea to send-ready brief
in under 60 seconds.

Creator briefs are where most brand collaborations fall apart too vague, too corporate, or too generic to produce content that actually looks like the brand. This tool generates a complete, specific, copy-paste-ready brief tailored to Qua's brand identity and the exact creator you're working with.

01
Configure the brief
Select creator type, the product being featured, the occasion, content format, and campaign goal. Add any specific notes about the creator or the shoot.
02
AI writes it for you
Claude generates a complete brief written in Qua's voice hook concepts, shot list, caption, do's & don'ts, hashtags, and usage rights. All specific. No filler.
03
Copy and send
Copy the full brief to your clipboard and paste it directly into an email or DM. Or regenerate with different parameters until it's exactly right.
