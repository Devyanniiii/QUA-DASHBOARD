Reputation Repair Protocol
Week-by-week · Who does what · What the AI does · What success looks like
W1This Week

Respond to the Google Review

The 3-year-old review scoring 9.4 damage must be answered by Rupanshi personally. Not a team member. Not a template. The founder.

Generate founder-voiced response using the AI engine below
Post response on Google — warm, genuine, no corporate language
Do not mention consumer forum — acknowledge the policy issue directly
End with a personal email address for direct follow up
AI-Powered
W2Week 2

Launch Post-Purchase Review Request Sequence

Set up a 3-email automated sequence sent to every customer 7, 14, and 21 days after purchase asking for a Google review.

Email 1 (Day 7): "How is your Qua piece settling in?" — casual, no ask yet
Email 2 (Day 14): Soft ask — "If you love it, we'd be grateful for a review"
Email 3 (Day 21): Direct link to Google review page — 2 clicks to review
Automated
W3–4Month 1

Answer Every Unanswered Mention

Work through the priority queue systematically. Reddit, Quora, Instagram comments, MouthShut. Every mention gets a response within 48 hours from this point forward.

Use AI response generator for each mention — personalise before posting
Set up Google Alerts for "Qua clothing" — catch new mentions in real time
Assign one team member as reputation monitor — 30 minutes daily
Team Task
Day 60Month 2

Target: 10 New Positive Google Reviews

The email sequence compounds. By day 60 with ~500 monthly customers, the sequence should generate 10–20 new reviews. The negative review begins to be buried.

Monitor review count weekly — target 3+ new reviews per week
Respond to every positive review too — shows the brand is alive and listening
Track search position of negative review — should move from #1 to #3+
Automated
Day 90Month 3

Reputation Health Score Target: 65+

With consistent response, new reviews flowing, and the SEO fixes live — the negative review no longer dominates the first search result page.

Run full audit — has the 9.4 damage score review dropped in search ranking?

The Stylist Strategy — Qua's Most Underused Lever
When Tamannaah or Rashmika gets dressed for a press event, their stylist chooses the brand — not them. Pitching celebrity stylists directly costs a fraction of paid celebrity partnerships and delivers organic, trusted placements. One stylist placement = earned media worth lakhs.
